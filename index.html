HOW TO MANAGE EXPENSES FOR LAZY PEOPLE

5 steps to get your personal finances together, January 19, 2019

Let me tell you, I am probably the laziest person on this planet. OK, I may be exaggerating, but I try living my life reaping maximum rewards for the least amount of effort.

I am going to share with you how I manage my monthly expenses with the least amount of effort.

Step 1 - Set up a bank account & download the app

It goes without saying, but set up a bank account and download your bank’s app on your smartphone. It makes it so much easier not be lazy. You can manage your account from your bed, from the toilet and from pretty much anywhere. Also, if you have an iPhone, you can log into your account with your fingerprints without having to type anything.
